Algebra

As a single word with an article or in the plural, "an algebra" or "algebras" denotes a specific mathematical structure, whose precise definition depends on the context. Usually, the structure has an addition, multiplication, and scalar multiplication . When some authors use the term "algebra", they make a subset associative, commutative, unital, and/or finite-dimensional, which allows for n-ary operations.
